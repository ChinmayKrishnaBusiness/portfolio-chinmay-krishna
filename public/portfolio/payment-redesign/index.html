How payment system changed in 1mg?

Before demonetization only Cash on delivery was available as payment mode on 1mg. After Demonetization, 1mg integrated online payment modes like paytm, razorpay and simpl in their payment page. So, people now had option to pay from wallets, cards, netbanking and UPI.

What was the problem with online payment gateways?

Payment gateway like Razorpay, provides way to pay by card, netbanking, wallet and UPI. But their technical failures was not in control of 1mg. Singnificant order loss was happening due to these failures.

Even Paytm had also significant failure rate.

Other problems

  • The UI changed after clicking on place order as you go to paytm or razorpay portal. So, it did not feel consistent in flow.
  • Longer flow for loyal and retained customer as no preffered and saved payment mode. This can lead to increased chance of dropoff during payment.

Solution

We will directly integrate wallets and cards in 1mg payments page through juspay (PCI DSS Level 1 compliant). People can now link wallets and save cards for faster checkout. During payments, UI style is of 1mg , giving consistent experience. Technically down modes will be upfront conveyed.

Inspiration & Analysis

We checked for famous consumer apps which have integrated payment modes

Mobile App

Amazon has saved payment methods at top and rest at below. It has two global CTAs.

Swiggy also has saved payment method at top and rest below. Though it has no global CTA but in-row CTAs.

Zomato shows only saved payment mode at first to reduce choice overload and to see all payment modes you click on "Add payment modes".

After analysis, we really liked Zomato's UX at payment step for app.

Desktop Web

Amazon has two Global CTAs -one for top fold conversion and other for bottom fold conversion. Current step is shown on stepper.

In Swiggy, first level navigation is type of payment and then inside selected type is the list. There is no global level CTA but in-row CTA like their app.

In Zomato, everything remains in first fold with all collapsed and selected type opened. They also have global CTA.

For web we really liked UI of Swiggy and its clarity. But we also needed Global CTA for 1mg payments with contextual copy like Zomato.

Design and Prototype

Mobile App

A new user who has no saved payment method will see it like this.

A repeated user who has saved a payment method will only see saved payment mode to reduce choice overload. If he wants to choose other mode, he can see full list of modes (saved and not saved)

Wallet

Wallets can be used by first linking it. Once it is linked it can be used for payment by just one click. Though sometimes wallet balance maybe low where he has to add money, but we have given quick amount suggestions to add.

Cards

Though in saved cards you have to enter CVV and OTP, and it is longer than 1 tap payment of linked wallet, it has less typing effort that not saved card.

Desktop Web

For desktop web, with more space, we kept navigational pattern to go to the required payment mode. Through navigational pattern we achieve progressive disclosure to achieve easy decision making and selection.
